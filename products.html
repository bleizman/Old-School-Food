Granola: Original Flavor

Our Original flavor granola is the prefect blend of spices balanced with almonds and hazelnuts and lightly sweetened with honey, creating a complex profile that calls out “yes, this is how granola should taste.”

The granola is crunchy and loose, not sticky and clumpy, making it the perfect, healthy snack throughout the day.

Our favorite ways to eat Old School Granola:

    For Breakfast: with plain Greek yogurt and a drizzle of maple syrup - you control the sweetness!

    For Cocktails: in place of beer nuts!

    For Dessert: as a topping to ice cream!

Mexican Hot Chocolate Granola

The ancient Mayans and Aztec believed chocolate was the drink of the gods. Old School Food has taken this sacred ingredient and mixed it with other traditional spices of Mexico: cinnamon and cayenne pepper to create our Mexican Hot Chocolate flavor. This Old School Food pays homage to the marriage of these traditional flavors to create a unique and delicious granola that can be enjoyed any time of day.
